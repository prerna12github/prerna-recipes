Mutton Biryani


Description

Mutton Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with tender mutton pieces, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of warm spices. It's a beloved comfort food that's perfect for special occasions or family gatherings.


Ingredients

  • 1 lb mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the mutton pieces and cook until they are browned and tender.
  3. Stir in the sliced onions and cook until they are translucent.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until they break down and form a sauce.
  5. Stir in the yogurt and spices, cooking for another minute.
  6. Add the basmati rice and mix well with the meat sauce.
  7. Transfer the mixture to a pot and add water, bringing it to a boil.
  8. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the rice is cooked through.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
