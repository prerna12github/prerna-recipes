Lasagna


Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Spread a third of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Pour a third of the remaining meat sauce over the cheese mixture.
  10. Repeat layers twice more.
  11. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  12. Bake for 30-35 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
